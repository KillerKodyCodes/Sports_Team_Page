Who We Are

Da Shed Armwrestling club was originally founded by amateur armwrestlers Will Smith and Kody Byrd of Hattiesburg, Mississippi. When the two realized that there was no regular practices which they could attend, they decided to begin practicing with each other in a shed in Will's back yard. One by one more and more amateurs began to practice with them. Thus, Da Shed Armwrestling Club was born. The club has since grown to having regular practices at Revolution Fitness with regular attendees including multiple state champions BJ Fokakis and Wayne Withers and World Champion Dustin Hyatt. The club's goal is to continue to foster amateur armwrestlers into the pros of tomorrow for Team Mississippi.

How It Works: club members are encouraged to make callouts to people either above them if they are already ranked, or people they believe they can beat if not already ranked. The rankings are handled on a case-by-case basis, and tournament results will not always affect the ordering or competitors directly. The preferred method is through supermatches.

Rules: Supermatches may be best of 3, best of 5, or first to 4 formats. All supermatches will follow IFA rules with at minimum one certified Head Referee.
